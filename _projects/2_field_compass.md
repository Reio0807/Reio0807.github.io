A single-file interactive visualization (D3 + Plotly) that maps the field I'm learning: foundational
safety work, AI × economics research, evaluation tooling, fellowships and funding, and the media that
tracks it all. Every item is tagged, relevance-scored, and annotated with *why it matters*. An
"under-explored zones" view crosses relevance against scarcity to surface candidate research
directions.

**Why it matters for safety:** building it forced me to actually read and place the literature, and
to think structurally about where the field's attention is — and isn't. It doubles as a public study
artifact and a research-direction generator.
