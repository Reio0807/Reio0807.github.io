A taxonomy of failure modes that only appear once you have many interacting AI agents — miscoordination, collusion, conflict, emergent dynamics no single agent intends. I found this clarifying because it gives safety vocabulary to things economists already study under other names (strategic interaction, market failure, mechanism design). It’s the clearest bridge I’ve found between my multi-agent LLM work and the safety literature: the same systems I build to simulate economies are instances of the risk surface this paper maps.
