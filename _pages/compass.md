The **Field Compass** is a hand-curated, interactive map of the territory I'm learning: foundational
AI-safety work, AI × economics research, evaluation methods and tooling, fellowships and funding, and
the media that tracks the field. Each item is tagged by subject, type, and year, scored for
relevance, and annotated with *why it matters*. The "underexplored zones" view highlights where
relevance is high but existing work is thin — candidate directions for new research.

It's a living document; I refresh it as the field moves. Use the lens, search, and chips to filter,
click a slice in the mind-map to drill in, and click an item to open its source.
