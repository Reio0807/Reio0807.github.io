## Orientation & foundations

- [Preventing an AI-related catastrophe](https://80000hours.org/problem-profiles/artificial-intelligence/) — 80,000 Hours' problem profile; the best single orientation to the field.
- [International AI Safety Report 2026](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.21012) — Bengio et al.; the authoritative global baseline on capabilities, risks, and safety.
- [Concrete Problems in AI Safety](https://arxiv.org/abs/1606.06565) — Amodei et al.; the foundational framing of empirical safety problems.
- [Human Compatible](https://people.eecs.berkeley.edu/~russell/hc.html) — Stuart Russell's framing of the alignment problem.
- [aisafety.com](https://aisafety.com/) — comprehensive community meta-index; a good launch page.

## AI evaluations & tooling

- [Evaluating Frontier Models for Dangerous Capabilities](https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.13793) — Phuong et al. (DeepMind); a reference point for capability evaluations.
- [Inspect AI](https://github.com/UKGovernmentBEIS/inspect_ai) — UK AISI's evaluation framework, with [200+ pre-built evals](https://github.com/UKGovernmentBEIS/inspect_evals).
- [METR Task Standard](https://github.com/METR/task-standard) — a schema for evaluating autonomous agents.
- [Model-Written Evaluations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.09251) — Anthropic; self-evaluation primitives.

## Alignment & oversight

- [Constitutional AI](https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.08073) — Anthropic's main alignment approach.
- [Weak-to-Strong Generalization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2312.09390) — Burns et al. (OpenAI); a foundational scalable-oversight result.
- [Scaling Laws for Scalable Oversight](https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.18530) — oversight under capability asymmetry, in scaling-law form.
- [Sleeper Agents](https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.05566) — Anthropic; deceptive alignment, empirically.

## AI governance & policy

- [GovAI research](https://www.governance.ai/research) — premier academic AI-governance center (Oxford).
- [UK AISI research agenda](https://www.aisi.gov.uk/research-agenda) — a government safety-institute's priorities.
- [CSET](https://cset.georgetown.edu/) — policy-side research with a strong technical-governance bridge.
- [AI Safety Index](https://futureoflife.org/ai-safety-index-winter-2025/) — independent assessment of frontier labs.

## AI × economics (my corner)

- [Homo Silicus](https://arxiv.org/abs/2301.07543) — Horton, Filippas & Manning; LLMs as computational subjects in economic experiments.
- [An Economy of AI Agents](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.01063) — Hadfield & Koh; an economist-facing survey of the agentic-AI landscape.
- [GPTs are GPTs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2303.10130) — Eloundou et al.; labor-market exposure to LLMs.
- [Power and Progress](https://www.basicbooks.com/titles/daron-acemoglu/power-and-progress/9781541702530/) — Acemoglu & Johnson; the political economy of automation.
- [Anthropic Economic Index](https://www.anthropic.com/research) — measuring AI's real economic footprint.
- [Causal Inference with LLMs: A Survey](https://aclanthology.org/2025.findings-naacl.327/) — Ma et al.; where causal inference meets language models.

## Multi-agent & agentic systems

- [Multi-Agent Risks from Advanced AI](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.14143) — Hammond et al.; a taxonomy of multi-agent risk.
- [Generative Agents](https://arxiv.org/abs/2304.03442) — Park et al. (Stanford); the architectural reference for multi-agent LLM simulation.
- [Strategic Collusion of LLM Agents](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.00031) — empirical evidence of LLM-agent collusion.
- [Anthropic Cookbook — agentic patterns](https://github.com/anthropics/anthropic-cookbook/tree/main/patterns/agents) — official building-block patterns.

## Programs, fellowships & funding

- [Anthropic Fellows Program](https://alignment.anthropic.com/2025/anthropic-fellows-program-2026/) — mentored frontier safety research.
- [MATS](https://www.matsprogram.org/) — mentored AI-safety apprenticeship (Berkeley / London).
- [SPAR](https://sparai.org/) — part-time mentored research, broad aperture.
- [BlueDot Impact](https://bluedot.org/) — structured AI-safety / AGI-strategy courses.
- [Open Philanthropy — Technical AI Safety RFP](https://coefficientgiving.org/request-for-proposals-technical-ai-safety-research) — funding directions and a useful map of priority areas.
- [Successif](https://successif.org/) — free career coaching for AI-safety transitions.
